    Milk Tea Smoothie Bowl

    Recipe by @mizhuhappy

    Milk tea smoothie bowl topped with tapioca pearls (Boba)

    Made with refined sugar, it includes hemp seeds to fulfil your daily intake of healthy omega fats.

    Recipe:

    1) Brew 2 x tea bags in hot milk of choice (I used soy milk) for at least 20 mins and set aside.

    2) Blend 4 x frozen bananas, 1 tablespoon of hemp seeds, 2 tablespoon of natvia sweetner and the brewed tea mix until it is thick and smooth.

    3) Scoop into a bowl and top with your fav toppings (tapioca peals are a must!)

    4) Enjoy!

    Feel free to add more tea for a stronger flavour.
